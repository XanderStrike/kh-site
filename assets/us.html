About Us

How it started

Our lives first intersected in a hotel lobby near Chicago’s O’Hare Airport in April 2003. Alana, a high-school junior, was applying for the BYFI summer program in Israel. Gabe, a college freshman and alumnus of the summer program, was helping out for the day -- greeting applicants while they waited for their interviews. We parted ways with a mutual crush.

Alana attended the program and started college in Chicago a year later. After that, our lives crossed paths periodically, most often at BYFI alumni events, sometimes in the home of Larry Edwards, a faculty member and (then) Rabbi at Congregation Or Chadash in Chicago.

We’re grateful to have Rabbi Edwards officiate our wedding.
