Fun around Portland

Planning a longer trip? We asked our family & friends for their Portland-area favorites…

Food & drink

  • Beer of all varieties
  • Breakfast burritos and salsa, with full-meat and full-vegan menus
  • Coffee, cocktails, and a slice
  • Voodoo donuts are world famous

Events

  • The World Naked Bike Ride is the day before the wedding (what a lucky coincidence!)
  • The Waterfront Blues Festival starts a few days later
  • Check out the Saturday Market
  • The Portland Timbers have a home game the following weekend

In the city

  • Explore the city via the 4T - Trail, Tram, Trolley, and Train! Includes beautiful views of the city on the Aerial Tram
  • Roses of all varieties at the International Rose Test Garden
  • Enjoy the world's smallest park
  • And Forest Park, one of the world's largest urban forest reserves
  • For shopping, dining, galleries and the like, check out 23rd St and the Pearl District in NW Portland.

Beyond the city

  • There are some beautiful hikes in the Golumbia River Gorge
  • Multnomah Falls is a must-see, though it will be crowded on the weekend
  • If it's hot, try the Oneonta Gorge hike for something different
  • Or take a day trip to Sauvie Island. In late June there should be great strawberries, lots of birds, warm beaches and a cool oak savannah.
