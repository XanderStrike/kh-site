Not a Portlander?

Book your room early! Portland gets lots of visitors in the summer.

Hotels

We have group rates at two hotels:
  • Benson Hotel until May 27
  • Mark Spencer until May 12

To get the rate, call before the cutoff date and mention that you're "with the Kinarsky / Rosenhouse wedding".

You might also look at the Hilton. The Jupiter is close to the venue, with a "young, hip vibe" -- there's a bar and music venue downstairs.

Look around for others.

Alternatives

You can rent a private room or a whole house on AirBnB or VRBO. We'll be staying a few nights in a small AirBnB in Northwest Portland.

Want to share housing with other guests from out-of-town? Send us a note, and we'll put you in touch: us@kin.house

Getting around

Public transit in Portland is called TriMet. It serves the airport (PDX) and has stops near the venue and all the hotels listed above. Get transit directions from Google Maps

If you're planning a longer stay, or want to see the famous outdoors of the Pacific Northwest, plan to rent a car at the airport or downtown
